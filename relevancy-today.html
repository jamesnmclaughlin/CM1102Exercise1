Relevancy Today

Grace Hopper lead the way for high level programming. Her work on COBOL helped and still helps companies to this day, produce understandable, yet efficient code. Not only this, but she helped produce the first electronic computer which allowed for new and improved computers to be built afterwards. The language that she created, helped popularise jobs and careers in computer programming. Because of the easy to understand code, it allowed many people to become proficient in programming as the code was simple structured english. Instead of taking hours to understand code or write code, people could easily pick up from where another programmer left off.

Her achievements have allowed industries to boom due to the rise in fast computers that can do calculations within a matter of seconds, reducing the need for humans to solve equations. This has created a world where computers have reduced the number of jobs available.
